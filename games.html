The Grief Cube

Point & Click Multiplayer Narrative Psychological

A co-op point-and-click experience about grief, played by two siblings navigating five rooms of shared loss.

Each room represents a stage of grief — Denial, Anger, Bargaining, Depression, and Acceptance. Players must solve puzzles together to advance, but grief is rarely agreed upon. Conflicts will arise.

The game is presented in first person. Players can see each wall of each room one by one, turning their head with arrows. What they find there depends on who they are, and what they've lost.

Genre Point & Click / Narrative
Players 2 (Online Co-op)
Perspective First Person
Target Release Before August 2026
Platform Windows 11
