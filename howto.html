// THE GRIEF CUBE

How to Play

Controls

The Grief Cube is designed to be played with a keyboard and mouse. Below is the full list of controls. Hover a row to highlight its key.

Key Action Description
Navigation
↑ Move to Next Navigate forward between the five stages of grief.
← → Look Left / Right Turn your head to view the left or right wall of the current room.
Interface
I Open Inventory Display collected items. Some objects are needed to solve puzzles in later rooms.
ESC Open Pause Menu Opens the pause menu.
