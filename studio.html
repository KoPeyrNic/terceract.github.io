// ABOUT

Built at EPITA Lyon

Terceract Games is a student game studio based at EPITA Lyon. We are five developers and artists brought together by a shared ambition: to create narrative games that explore difficult human experiences with honesty and craft.

Our debut title, The Grief Cube, is a co-op point-and-click game about grief, expected to release before August 2026.
